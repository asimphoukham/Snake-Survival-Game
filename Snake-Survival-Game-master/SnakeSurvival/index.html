Snake Survival

Rules

CONTROLS

Use the left, right, up, and down keys on your keyboard to move the snake.

HOW TO PLAY
1. Eat the red apples to earn points.
2. If you collide into any side of the wall or any part of your own snake, then "GAME OVER".
3. Beware of the black apples! Those are the "Apples of Death." Each time you eat one, it will deduct 1 point. If you're down to 0 points and eat a black apple..."GAME OVER!"

HAVE FUN!
