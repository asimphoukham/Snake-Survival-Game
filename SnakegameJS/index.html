Rules

Controls

Use the left, right, up and down keys to move the snake.

How to Play

1. Eat the red apples to earn points.

2. Your snake will grow as you eat apples.

3. If you collide into any side of the wall or any part of your own snake then "GAME OVER!"

4. Beware of the black apples! Those are the "Apples of Death." Each time you eat one it'll deduct 1 point. If you're down to 0 points and eat a black apple..."GAME OVER!"

Have fun!
